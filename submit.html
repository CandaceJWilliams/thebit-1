Share Your Story

How to pitch to us:

Email your story idea, plus a short introduction of yourself, to: editor@women2.com

Examples of stories we love:

  • Personal accounts of women’s entrepreneurial or leadership success
  • Personal accounts of women’s entrepreneurial or leadership struggles and challenges
  • Trend reports on new advancements in technology
  • Insights on entrepreneurship or tech investing
  • Workplace reflections
  • Essays on the tech industry, the world of work, gender equality
  • Cultural discussions on gender or diversity
  • Profiles of/interviews with leaders in tech
  • Helpful, unique advice
  • Tech industry news

NOTE:

We work hard for women in the tech industry by providing solutions that close gender gaps in tech, and we offer several ways for brands to access our audience through our media property. If you’re writing on behalf a brand or have goals to give your company exposure past your own thought leadership, please visit our brand engagement page to learn more about sponsored content with Women 2.0. We’d be pleased to work with you!
